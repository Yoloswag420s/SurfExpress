About SurfExpress

Our Mission

We are dynamic multimedia website dedicated to both novice and experienced surfers. This platform will serve as a comprehensive resource for learning to surf, featuring essential information about the sport, a directory of surf hotspots worldwide, and a forum for users to connect and discuss all things related to surfing. Additionally, the site will provide valuable insights into surf culture, gear recommendations.

Brand Vision

As a website dedicated to surfers of all kinds the main purpose and vision is to create a thriving community and hub for all surfers to allow for the collective of necessary information to introduce and keep the surfing community active with the help of an online environment.

Our Values

To be Welcoming, Passionate, and Inclusive.
