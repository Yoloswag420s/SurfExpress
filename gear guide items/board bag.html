Board Bag

Purpose

A surfboard bag is more than just a convenient way to carry your board—it's essential for keeping it protected from dings, UV rays, and other elements that can damage it over time. Whether you're traveling by car or plane, your board is constantly at risk of scratches and pressure dings, especially if it's loosely packed or exposed to harsh conditions. A good board bag shields your board from impacts and also helps keep it cool under the sun, which is crucial for preserving its integrity.

What to Look For

When selecting a board bag, look for thick padding (at least 5–10mm), reinforced stitching, and corrosion-resistant zippers. A quality bag will also offer extra features like internal storage pockets, padded shoulder straps, and ventilation panels to prevent moisture buildup. Make sure the size matches your board—too tight can be damaging, while too loose can cause it to shift around. Investing in a sturdy, well-designed board bag not only extends the life of your surfboard but gives you peace of mind on every trip.
