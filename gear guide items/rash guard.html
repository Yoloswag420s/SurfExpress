RashGuard

Purpose

A rash guard is a lightweight, form-fitting shirt designed to protect your skin while surfing. It's especially useful for preventing chafing caused by rubbing against the surfboard or wetsuit, and it offers an extra layer of protection from the sun's harmful UV rays. Many surfers wear rash guards during warm-weather sessions when a full wetsuit isn’t necessary but sun and friction protection still are.

What to Look For

When choosing a rash guard, prioritize fabric quality and fit. Look for UV-rated, quick-drying materials with flatlock stitching to minimize irritation. A snug but flexible fit will ensure it stays in place even through wipeouts and paddling. Long-sleeve options provide maximum sun protection, while short-sleeve versions offer a lighter, cooler feel. For extra grip, some rash guards come with boardshort connectors to keep everything secure during movement.
